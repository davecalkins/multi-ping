MultiPing

MultiPing is a multi-protocol, multi-host, graphical ping utility written in Java.  Current supported protocols are ICMP echo, HTTP, SMTP, and POP.  This goes beyond simply monitoring by ICMP echo and allows status monitoring of whether your servers are up and providing the expected services.

See the download page to obtain MultiPing.
